Voltage regulator: I went through multiple resources online and had a discussion with the team about it , finally the goal set was to read more about creating a step-down voltage regulator for our circuit, as we are planning to utilize this for converting 10V/12V to 5V for our ESP32 and even 10V/12V to 5V for our programmable Led. We are currently keeping them on two different power lines, so the ESP32 is operatable even when the user chooses the turn off the LEDs analogous. I took the liberty of researching and pitching of choosing a voltage regulator instead of a divider because, using voltage divider will involve using large value resistors and is 80% less efficient under load. We are not considering a buck converter because we are planning on choosing a wall plug that converts ac to dc.
